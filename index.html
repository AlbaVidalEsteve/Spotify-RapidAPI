Welcome to MusicHub

Discover new music based on your favorite songs from your public Spotify playlists.

Instructions:

  1. Log in to your Spotify account.
  2. Navigate to your public playlists.
  3. Select the tracks you want to use for recommendations.
  4. Click the "Get Recommendations" button.
  5. Enjoy your personalized music recommendations!

Make sure your Spotify playlists are set to public for this app to access them.
